HopHelper Features Overview

ABV & Attenuation Calculator

Calculate key values for your brew, such as Alcohol By Volume (ABV), Alcohol By Weight (ABW), real and apparent attenuation, and more.

Beer Color Wheel

Determine your beer's color using the SRM scale, helping you stay within your desired beer style.

Mash Thickness Calculator

Find the perfect mash thickness ratio based on the type of beer you're brewing, ensuring optimal extraction.

Alpha Acid Calculator

Adjust your hop usage based on available alpha acid levels to keep your recipe consistent.

Color Converter

Convert between color units like SRM, EBC, and Lovibond to get the exact shade you want for your brew.

Sugars Converter

Seamlessly convert between Plato, Brix, and Specific Gravity to simplify your measurements.

Liquid Alcohol Mixer

Calculate the final alcohol level when mixing different liquids, useful for blending and mixology.

Sugar Temperature Corrector

Correct sugar measurement values for temperature variations to ensure precise readings.

Liquid Converter

Convert between various volume units, including standard and brewery-specific units like kegs and bottles.

Length Converter

Switch between common length units, such as meters, feet, and inches, useful for ingredient and equipment measurements.

Temperature Converter

Easily convert between Celsius, Fahrenheit, and Kelvin for accurate temperature control.

Weight Converter

Convert between weight units, including grams, ounces, pounds, and kilograms, essential for precise ingredient usage.

Beer & Food Pairing

Get personalized suggestions for beer and food pairings to enhance your overall experience.
